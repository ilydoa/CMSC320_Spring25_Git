Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: Git helps us by giving us a version control system, so that we can keep track of our past 
changes, find inconsistencies, and work in a team without accidentally overwriting teammates' 
work.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Data science could be applied to the field of medicine, where it could be used to find 
patterns at a large scale or higher level of diseases across patients.
